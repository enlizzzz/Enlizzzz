I'm the lead animator for the short film Relive by Yumcha Production, my final year project.
Over time, I've proven myself capable of leading teams and adapting to different roles,
from acting for references and suggesting shots to compositing, 2D & 3D animation, and logo design.

One of my proudest moments was seeing my team's film theme get selected.
It meant that our creative vision resonated, and my contributions
whether in storytelling, animation, or teamwork
helped shape a project strong enough to stand out.
This experience reinforced my ability to collaborate, adapt, and bring ideas to life.

Another proud moment in my artistic journey is when I was teaching art students.
I helped a student overcome his lack of interest in making a collage by
holding up the collage paper that matched his height.
He would paste pieces while playing like he was shooting a basketball.
Teaching students has sparked my creativity, inspiring me to create more,
take on more ambitious projects, and find greater purpose.
I love creating and I love cats too.
